## My mindset

> "Real value in IT comes from understanding problems, empathizing with customer pains and addressing them using the right tools at the right time."

Software development is a mean to solve real-life problems, making our lives easier and more enjoyable - freeing up time for us to do things that matter and reducing overhead and repetitive tasks. Software Engineering is the discipline where we are able to apply this at scale (both temporal and size scales) and finding the right tools to do the job and bringing more value to our customers and users.

My mission as a Software Engineer (both an individual contributor as well as a manager) is to understand real world problems, figure out means to solve those problems using the tools at our disposal, coach myself and my team mates to improve engineering practices, understand our customer's pains and ambitions and bringing this all together in incremental deliveries that add up value to our customers and allow our teams to grow as engineers.
